About

Commercial clarity in
complex systems.

My background spans wholesale electricity markets and retail transformation inside a large corporate, climate-tech start-ups, and an investor-backed distributed energy portfolio. Across all of it, the work has been the same: make complex decisions clearer and turn them into accountable delivery.

I've led physical energy trading across a ~10 GW integrated portfolio, built new business units from scratch, driven pre-Series A fundraising, and overseen vendor governance on a A$300m retail transformation. I bring an actuarial foundation to every problem — rigorous with numbers, honest about uncertainty.

Currently focused on practical governance in the net-zero transition. When I'm not working, I'm probably somewhere remote — I completed a 50 km ultramarathon in the Canadian Rockies and trained Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in Rio during a 10-month sabbatical across four continents.

Career highlights

General Management · Distributed Energy

Governance · Delivery

Vendor Governance Lead — AGL Retail Transformation

Led vendor governance and delivery assurance during the foundational phase of AGL's A$300m Retail Transformation with Kaluza (the UK platform behind OVO Energy's 6m+ meters). Managed a team including Baringa Partners consultants.

Start-up · Strategy
2020 – 2023

Chief Strategy Officer & Chief of Staff — UPowr

Led commercial design securing A$950k through the Entrepreneurs' Programme and an ARENA-supported project. Drove pre-Series A preparation — co-built the financial model, ran the investor pipeline, and represented the company in the AWS Clean Energy Accelerator.

Energy Trading · Markets · Analytics
2013 – 2020

Progressed from Load Forecasting Analyst through Commercial Analyst (New Energy) and Pricing & Strategy Lead to Electricity Physical Markets Trader — owning real-time dispatch, bidding and gas nominations across AGL's ~10 GW integrated portfolio with direct P&L accountability under live risk and compliance constraints.
