About

Neuroscience of brand and belonging

I started Mediakit after years of working inside large agencies and realizing that the most powerful brands aren't built on tactics — they're built on truth. The truth of what a company actually believes, and why that belief matters to the people it serves.

My background spans consumer psychology, creative direction, and strategic communication. I've worked with Series A startups, heritage companies, and independent founders across four countries.

When I'm not building brands, I'm reading philosophy, travelling, or thinking about why some ideas spread and others don't.
